Having determined that you should specialize in gathering firewood, find a trade that will make you and Friday better off.

Recall that Friday produces 225 calories of fish per hour or 4 logs of firewood per hour and to survive, he needs 40 logs and 450 calories of fish. while you produce 250 calories or 8 logs per hour and to survive you must collect 32 logs and 2000 calories of fish.
